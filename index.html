We serve our community

Every year Lambda Chi Alpha collects hundreds of thousands of pounds of cans for Feeding America. As a national fraternity, we have raised over 10 million pounds since 2013. Within our local chapter, we participate in the annual St. Baldricks competition which benefits childhood cancer research. To find out more about our philanthropy, click here.

Who is Lambda Chi Alpha
Beta Kappa?

The Beta Kappa chapter of Lambda Chi Alpha was established in 1942, but has much deeper roots within the Georgia Tech community. Established with the purpose of recruiting the finest gentlemen and guiding them on their journey through university, Beta Kappa has evolved into the premier fraternity on campus, providing a Lifetime of Brotherhood to over 2000 gentlemen.
