Standards Chair

Standards Chair works within two areas of the fraternity, the officers and the brothers. In respect to the officers, he acts as the quality assurance for every officer, making sure each and every officer is performing his duty to the best of his abilities (and working with them to increase their abilities). In terms of the brotherhood, the Standards Chair oversees and maintains the various forms of communication (email, GroupMe, etc.).
